# ConfidPay

Encrypted payroll, programmable treasury outflows, and contractor payments where amounts, recipients, vesting schedules, and milestone conditions stay fully encrypted during computation.

Built on **Fhenix CoFHE** + **Privara SDK**.

## Why ConfidPay?

Public blockchains expose every salary, bonus, contractor payout, and treasury movement. This leads to:

- **Social attacks** and doxxing risks
- **Tax leakage** from predictable on-chain patterns
- **MEV front-running** on recurring payment flows
- **Compliance barriers** for institutions and RWA players

ConfidPay solves this by making confidentiality the foundational architecture — not a bolt-on feature.
